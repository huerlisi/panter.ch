Services

Services

We’re professionals in Ruby on Rails, Java & JEE, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript frameworks like JQuery, Backbone and Spine just to name a few.

We supply consulting and solutions in agile project management and Enterprise Java. Our Teams not only teach but also master Spring, Java EE, OSGi and Software Engineering and provide Best Practice and offers from order processing to the completion of your mobile iOS and Android Page.

Our work is behaviour and test driven.

Our solutions run in the cloud, your dedicated environment or your own infrastructure.

Applications for your consumers

Our customers grow their business, engage their customers and corner the market in their field — all in one and online. Work with us, explain your vision and watch us build your custom public solution that brings your idea to life.

Applications for your workforce

We develop enterprise and internal applications used at companies around the globe. Some optimize workflows. Some help ensuring process quality. Others keep it simple, like letting you schedule a conference room.

Mobile optimized websites

Users need to have a good experience when accessing your site via mobile. And you can give it to them. The best part? You can give it to everyone — no matter what device they’re using. That’s the beauty of mobile optimized applications that we deliver.
